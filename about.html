Our Sweet Story

Discover the passion, craftsmanship, and dedication behind every DivineByte creation

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010 by pastry chef Isabella Rossi, DivineByte Desserts began as a small home-based bakery with a simple mission: to create extraordinary desserts that bring joy to every celebration.

What started with just three cake recipes and a dream has blossomed into a premier luxury confectionery, serving clients across the country with our signature creations.

Today, our team of 15 passionate pastry chefs continues Isabella's legacy, combining traditional techniques with innovative flavors to craft desserts that are as beautiful as they are delicious.

Meet Our Team

The talented individuals behind our sweet creations

Chef Ayesha Khan

Founder & Head Pastry Chef

With over a decade of experience in Karachi’s finest bakeries, Chef Ayesha Khan transformed her love for artful desserts into DivineByte Desserts — a brand inspired by Pakistan’s warmth, flavor, and culture. From local celebrations to corporate events, her creative touch and dedication bring sweetness to every occasion.

Chef Ahmed Khan

Executive Pastry Chef

Known for his creativity and precision, Chef Ahmed Khan has mastered the art of blending traditional Pakistani sweetness with contemporary dessert styles. From the heart of Karachi’s local bakeries to our modern studio kitchen, he brings innovation, passion, and authentic local taste to every creation.

Chef Ayesha Malik

Cake Design Specialist

Known for her artistic vision and precision, Chef Ayesha Malik is the creative mind behind our most stunning cake designs. Inspired by Karachi’s vibrant culture and colors, she blends traditional Pakistani artistry with modern dessert styling to craft cakes that are truly one-of-a-kind.

Chef Bilal Hussain

Chocolate & Confectionery Expert

A master chocolatier from Karachi, Chef Bilal Hussain is known for his skill in chocolate tempering and sugar craft. His creations combine rich Pakistani cocoa flavors with refined European techniques — turning every dessert into an edible masterpiece.

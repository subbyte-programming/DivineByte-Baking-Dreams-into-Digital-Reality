Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I place my order?

For standard cakes, we recommend ordering at least 3-5 days in advance. For custom designs, wedding cakes, or large orders, we suggest placing your order 2-4 weeks ahead of time to ensure availability and proper preparation.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery within a 20-mile radius of our bakery. Delivery fees start at $15 and vary based on distance and order size. For wedding cakes and large orders, we provide specialized delivery and setup services.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your order up to 48 hours before the scheduled pickup/delivery date for a full refund. For cancellations within 48 hours, a 50% cancellation fee applies. Wedding cake cancellations require 30 days notice for a full refund.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer a variety of options including gluten-free, vegan, nut-free, and dairy-free cakes. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when placing your order so we can accommodate your needs.

Can I customize a cake design?

Yes! We specialize in custom cake designs. You can bring in photos, themes, or ideas, and our talented pastry chefs will work with you to create a unique masterpiece for your special occasion. Custom designs may require a consultation.
